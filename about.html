About PaySide

Professional payroll umbrella solutions

Our Story

Founded with a vision to simplify payroll for contractors and agencies, PaySide Solutions has grown to become a trusted umbrella company serving thousands across the UK.

We understand that contracting is more than just timesheets. It is about compliance, tax efficiency, and peace of mind. Our mission is to provide professional umbrella services that empower contractors to focus on their work whilst we handle PAYE, tax filing, and all administrative complexities.

We have refined our processes and technology to deliver fast payments, accurate tax calculations, and exceptional customer support.
